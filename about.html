ABOUT US

Who We Are

Dream Big Project is a member-owned, digital cooperative ecosystem designed to support long-term dream execution for personal, family, NGO, and community benefit.

At Dream Big Project, we responsibly pool member contributions, provide education and mentorship, and apply cooperative principles to help members prepare for and execute meaningful projects.

Our Vision

A world where disciplined dreams grow into sustainable realities through integrity, cooperation, and shared prosperity.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities to responsibly execute meaningful dreams through structured contributions, cooperative collaboration, mentorship, clear timelines, and accountability.
