Dream Big Project — Orientation (MoU)

1. Purpose of this MoU

Dream Big Project exists as a structured execution ecosystem designed to help individuals and communities move from ideas and dreams to disciplined, funded, and responsibly executed outcomes.

This MoU establishes clarity, accountability, and mutual understanding between DBP and its Members.

2. Nature of Dream Big Project

Dream Big Project is NOT: An investment platform; a get-rich-quick scheme; a donation-for-profit program; a financial guarantee system.

Dream Big Project IS: A structured development and execution ecosystem; a discipline-driven contribution & learning platform; a long-term capacity-building system. No profits, returns, or outcomes are guaranteed.

3. Membership & Eligibility

Membership is open to individuals who agree to follow the Dream Big process. Progression is based on discipline, consistency, participation, and learning engagement. Mere registration does not guarantee access to funding or execution privileges.

4. Contributions & Financial Commitment

Member contributions are commitment-based, not investments. Contributions support financial discipline, long-term planning, and execution readiness. Contributions are not instant-withdrawal funds. Contribution rules, schedules, and usage are clearly defined and communicated.

5. Non-Refund Policy

Contributions are generally non-refundable, except where DBP explicitly states otherwise. Contributions reflect long-term commitment, not short-term liquidity. Members acknowledge that withdrawal requests may be denied based on policy.

6. Maturity & Waiting Period

Dream Big Project operates a 24-month maturity cycle before project funding eligibility. This period exists to build financial intelligence, emotional discipline, and execution readiness. No exceptions are guaranteed.

7. Dream Classroom (Compulsory)

All Members must participate in the Dream Classroom. This includes training in financial intelligence, money management, and strategic execution. Failure to engage may delay or revoke progression privileges.

8. Project Funding & Execution

Funding access is conditional, not automatic. Approval depends on discipline record, classroom engagement, contribution consistency, and execution readiness. DBP reserves the right to delay, modify, or decline project execution.

9. Multiple Project Participation

Members may subscribe to multiple project categories. Each project requires separate discipline and separate contribution responsibility. Failure in one project does not automatically affect others unless stated.

10. Referrals

Referrals are optional, not compulsory. Progress is not dependent on recruitment. No member is penalized for non-referral.

11. Transparency & Communication

DBP commits to transparent communication. Members commit to staying informed through official channels. Ignorance of communicated policies is not an excuse.

12. Accountability & Discipline

Members are accountable for their actions, commitments, and conduct. DBP may impose delays, restrictions, suspension, or termination for misconduct or violation of this MoU.

13. Inactivity

Prolonged inactivity may result in loss of eligibility, reset of progress, or removal from active programs.

14. Termination

DBP reserves the right to terminate membership for breach of agreement, fraud, misrepresentation, or disruptive behavior. Termination does not automatically entitle refunds.

15. No Partnership or Employment

This MoU does not create employment, partnership, agency, or joint venture. Members participate independently.

16. Limitation of Liability

Dream Big Project shall not be liable for personal financial decisions, member negligence, or external economic factors.

17. Amendments

DBP reserves the right to amend this MoU with notice. Continued participation signifies acceptance.

18. Governing Principles

This MoU is governed by integrity, accountability, discipline, and ethical execution.

19. Acceptance

By registering, signing digitally, or continuing participation, the Member confirms acceptance of the Dream Big Project Memorandum of Understanding.
