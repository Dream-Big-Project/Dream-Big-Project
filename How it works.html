Process highlights

• Join the Cooperative Ecosystem.
• Eligible individuals (18+)
• Choose a Member Tier Contribution Path that suits you.
• Select either a one-time contribution (Active Member) or a structured monthly contribution over a defined period (Passive Member).
• Select a Dream Project.

Development & Support

• Learning & Preparation Phase — training, mentorship, and planning while waiting for Dream Project Maturity (Dream Classroom).
• Project Review & Maturity — projects follow structured readiness & review process.
• Project Execution Support — approved projects are supported and funded through cooperative resources & Foundation support, subject to governance review and ecosystem capacity.
Important Clarification
• Dream Big Ecosystem is not an investment platform.
• Does not guarantee funding amounts or timelines.
• Does not promise profits, returns, or payouts.
• All project support is participatory, conditional, and governance-approved.
